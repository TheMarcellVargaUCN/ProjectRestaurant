About us

The story of Svalegaarden in Hasseris

In 1952, the beautiful grocery farm was rebuilt on the corner of Thorsens Allé and Svalegårdsvej, and the farm was also renamed Svalegaarden. The idyllic farm always stands at the same address, and has set the stage for many events and experiences over the years.

In June 2016, Svalegaarden got new tenants with a new spirit and bright hopes for the future - Mette and Stefan from the former Restaurant Mest.

Mette and Stefan come with new strength and fresh eyes on the beautiful renovated grocery farm, which has so far functioned as a company business.

Mette and Stefan have a vision to take the best from Restaurant Mest and continue it in the farm's beautiful setting in the heart of Aalborg. The thoughts behind Svalegaarden Most are gastronomy meets rural idyll, where the focus is on creating memorable experiences.
